The Details

Everything you need to know. Nothing you don't.

Guest Capacity

Sea Glass Venue welcomes intimate gatherings of 20 guests to celebrations of up to 150. Our space adapts to your vision, whether you're planning a quiet elopement with your closest circle or a full reception under the stars.

The ceremony lawn comfortably seats 150 with open-air views. The covered pavilion hosts up to 120 for seated dining, or 150 for cocktail-style receptions. For smaller weddings, we offer a more intimate courtyard setting that feels like a private garden by the sea.

We host one wedding per day. The venue is yours from morning preparation through your final dance.

Seasons

Pawleys Island is beautiful year-round, each season offering its own character.

Spring (March–May) — Mild temperatures, blooming sea oats, and the longest golden hours. Our most popular season.

Summer (June–August) — Warm evenings, ocean breezes, and dramatic sunsets. Evening ceremonies recommended.

Autumn (September–November) — Crisp air, softer light, and fewer crowds. A hidden gem for those in the know.

Winter (December–February) — Quiet, intimate, and unexpectedly romantic. The coast stripped to its essence.

Rain Plan

Coastal weather has a mind of its own. We're ready for it.

Our covered pavilion offers the same sweeping views as the outdoor ceremony lawn just with a roof overhead. Floor-to-ceiling openings let the breeze through while keeping you dry. Many couples tell us their rain plan reception was even more magical than they'd imagined.

We monitor weather closely in the days leading up to your event and work with you to make the call at the right moment. No rushing. No stress. Just a beautiful day, whatever the sky decides.

Accessibility

We believe every guest should experience your celebration fully and comfortably.

All primary spaces at Sea Glass Venue are wheelchair accessible, including the ceremony lawn, pavilion, restrooms, and both preparation suites. Paved pathways connect all buildings, and accessible parking is located near the main entrance.

We're happy to discuss specific accommodations during your tour. Please let us know how we can best support you and your guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend reaching out 12 to 18 months ahead for peak season dates (spring and early fall). However, we occasionally have openings for couples with shorter timelines it never hurts to ask.

Do you provide catering and vendors?

We have a curated list of preferred caterers, florists, photographers, and musicians who know our space well. You're also welcome to bring your own vendors we're flexible as long as they carry the required insurance.

Can we host the ceremony and reception on-site?

Absolutely. Most of our couples choose to hold everything here ceremony on the lawn or beach, cocktails on the terrace, and reception in the pavilion. It keeps the day seamless and stress-free.

Is there lodging nearby for guests?

Pawleys Island and the surrounding Lowcountry offer a range of options, from charming bed-and-breakfasts to beachfront vacation rentals. We're happy to share our recommendations based on your group size.

What's included in the venue rental?

Your rental includes exclusive use of all venue spaces for the full day, setup and breakdown time, tables and chairs, a dedicated venue coordinator, on-site parking for guests, and access to the bridal and groom suites.

Are there noise restrictions?

Amplified music may continue until 10:00 PM to respect our neighbors and the natural quiet of the coast. After that, you're welcome to keep the celebration going with acoustic music or conversation under the stars.
