The Day

A rhythm, not a schedule

We don't rush. We don't over-plan. We let the day breathe.

Arrival

The venue opens its doors. Coffee brews. Hair and makeup begin in the bridal suite while groomsmen settle into the cottage. The day starts slow, unhurried.

Ceremony

Guests gather on the lawn or by the shore. The breeze carries music. Vows are exchanged with sand between toes and salt on lips. Intimate. Genuine.

Cocktail Hour

Guests wander the terrace. Oysters are shucked. Local wine pours. The photographer captures candid joy while the sun begins its descent.

Reception

Toasts under string lights. First dances on weathered wood floors. The night unfolds at its own pace laughter, tears, and music drifting out over the water.
